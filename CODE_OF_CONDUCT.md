# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in 75 Club a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Standards

**We expect contributors to:**

- Be respectful of differing opinions and experience levels
- Give and receive constructive feedback graciously
- Take responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Prioritise what is best for the community and the project

**We do not tolerate:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks in any form
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwanted advances
- Deliberately derailing discussions or reviews
- Publishing others' private information without their explicit consent
- Conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Git & Review Standards

Beyond interpersonal conduct, we hold contributors to these collaborative standards:

- **Do not force-push to a shared or open PR branch.** It destroys history for everyone collaborating on that branch and invalidates existing review comments.
- **Do not rebase an open PR branch.** Rebasing changes commit SHAs and makes the review diff unreadable. Merge `main` in instead.
- **PRs must include screenshots or a recording for any UI change.** Reviewers cannot meaningfully evaluate visual changes without seeing them.

Violations of these standards — especially repeated ones — may result in contributions being closed without merge.
